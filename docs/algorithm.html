Algorithm

Our project's goal

Our project wants to answer this problem: How much joy was offered by any of the teams to their fans during the football season during a specific period? (How much joy did the team give to their fans during a home game during a specific period?) This is the problem we intend to solve.

Example Calculation

For example, if Team A won its first home game by 2-1, the JoyPoints offered to their fans are:

2 (1 for each goal) + 5 (for the win) = 7 in total.

The sum of these points adds to the total number of JoyPoints for each season. And if we sum up the JoyPoints for a period of 5 years (I will probably add two more seasons in the dataset to have a round number), we will have a clear view of Superettan’s joy to their fans!

To make our enquiry more specific we put some ranges that put the teams, based on their score, into a certain category.

The categories are five, from A to E and they go as follows:

Category JoyPoints
A ≥ 300
B 250-299
C 150-249
D 51-149
E 0-50

For example, if the score is equal or more than 300, the team will be included in category A. The Super joyous fans!
On the other hand, if the score is below or equal to 50, then something would have gone wrong and the team will be included in category E, the poor, poor and miserable fans…
